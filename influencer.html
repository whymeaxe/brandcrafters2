Common Questions

Influencer marketing
explained simply.

How do you choose the right influencer for my brand? +
We screen influencers based on niche relevance, audience demographics, engagement quality (not just follower count), content style, and brand safety. We then shortlist candidates, share profiles for your approval, and only proceed with creators who are genuinely aligned with your brand values and audience.
Do you work with nano and micro-influencers or only large accounts? +
We work across all tiers. Nano (1K–10K) and micro (10K–100K) influencers often deliver significantly higher engagement and stronger purchase intent for local businesses compared to mega-influencers. We match the tier to your campaign goal — reach vs. conversion vs. awareness — not just the biggest number we can find.
I'm a creator. How do I know if I'm ready for the Creator Growth Program? +
If you're consistently posting content, have any following at all, and want to turn your passion into paid collaborations, you're ready. We work with creators at all stages — from those just starting out to those with established audiences who want to monetise more effectively and professionally.
What is a media kit and why do I need one as a creator? +
A media kit is a professional document that presents your profile, audience demographics, engagement stats, content portfolio, and collaboration rates to potential brand partners. Without one, brands have no structured way to evaluate you. With a professional media kit, you instantly look credible and ready for business.
How long does an influencer campaign typically take? +
Planning and creator selection takes 1–2 weeks. Content creation and approval adds another 1–2 weeks. Publishing and campaign duration varies — typically 2–4 weeks for a full campaign cycle. We can also manage ongoing, always-on ambassador-style programs for brands that want consistent influencer coverage.
